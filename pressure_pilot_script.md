# PRESSURE - PILOT EPISODE
**Urban Thriller Series | 9:16 Vertical Format | High-Tension Thriller Vibe**

**Scene 1: INT. MINIMALIST BEDROOM - 06:27 AM**
Extreme close-up of eyes flickering open in darkness, a light leak effect washing over the frame. A digital clock glows "06:27 AM". A man in his 30s jolts awake and slaps the alarm. The room is sleek, modern, and cool-toned.

> **RADIO ANNOUNCER (V.O.)**
> *(Energetic, FM radio voice)*
> Good morning, City! You know the voice— Traffic building on the Eastway…

---

**Scene 2: INT. BATHROOM / KITCHEN - MORNING ROUTINE**
Fast montage: The BUSINESSMAN moves efficiently. He pours coffee, knots a sharp tie in the bathroom mirror. He is composed, focused. A close-up of his luxury wristwatch shows the second hand ticking relentlessly. The reflection in the watch glass shows his intense, focused eyes.

> **NARRATOR (V.O.)**
> Average morning. But time is always ticking.

---

**Scene 3: INT. KITCHEN ISLAND - 06:47 AM**
The Businessman stands at a sleek kitchen island, phone to his ear. A second phone on the counter lights up with an INCOMING CALL. He swaps calls calmly, his face illuminated by the screen showing "06:47".

> **BUSINESSMAN**
> *(Calm, authoritative)*
> Send the file to— Ten minutes. Same spot.

> **NARRATOR (V.O.)**
> Meet confirmed. Late.

---

**Scene 4: INT. CORRIDOR - COUNTDOWN MONTAGE**
Hyper-fast montage: Shower steam, a tailored suit jacket being pulled on, silver cufflinks snapping into place, keys grabbed, a leather wallet pocketed. He moves through a modern corridor with a speed ramp effect, motion blur trailing behind him.

> **NARRATOR (V.O.)**
> Every second counts.

---

**Scene 5: INT. LUXURY CAR / GARAGE - DEPARTURE**
Wide shot of an immaculate modern living room as smart home lights chase toward the garage. The garage door opens remotely. Inside the luxury car, a side profile of the Businessman. The engine starts. Dashboard lights reflect across his face. A steady bass pulse and persistent ticking fill the air.

> **NARRATOR (V.O.)**
> The machine wakes up.

---

**Scene 6: EXT. DRIVEWAY GATE - SLOW MOTION**
Slow motion: The luxury car ascends a steep driveway. An automatic iron gate slides open. The scene has a cool color grade with golden highlights gleaming on the car's chrome. The gate motor hums heavily.

> **NARRATOR (V.O.)**
> Breathe. Now.

---

**Scene 7: EXT. STREET - THE TRAP**
Drone down-angle view. Suddenly, unmarked black cars appear from both sides of the street, boxing the luxury car in. Tires screech violently. Interior POV: The Businessman makes a quick calculation. His hand grips the gear shift. A micro head tilt.

> **BUSINESSMAN**
> *(Whisper, cold)*
> Not today.

---

**Scene 8: EXT. STREET - ESCAPE**
Handheld low-angle shot. Hard cut: The luxury car threads through a narrow gap between the unmarked cars at high speed. The engine roars. Mirrors brush against each other. Rear mount view looking back: The unmarked cars collide lightly as chaos builds.

> **NARRATOR (V.O.)**
> Pressure makes diamonds. Or it crushes you.

---

**Scene 9: EXT. STREET - TITLE CARD**
The luxury car speeds off into the distance, leaving the chaos behind. The screen cuts to BLACK. Silence. A single, loud watch tick echoes.

> **NARRATOR (V.O.)**
> PRESSURE.
