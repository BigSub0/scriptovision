## STEP 5 — ADDING THE VOICEOVER IN CAPCUT

Once all clips are assembled in order, add your voiceover last:

1. In CapCut, tap **Audio → Record** (or import a pre-recorded MP3)
2. Record or import each segment's narration using the voiceover script from `thornton_wildcats_documentary_FULL.md`
3. Sync each narration segment to its corresponding video segment using the voiceover cues listed above
4. Adjust clip lengths to match your narration timing — the durations above are estimates
5. Add subtle background music underneath the voiceover at about 15-20% volume

**Voice recommendation:** Deep, measured, authoritative — like a Ken Burns documentary. Morgan Freeman style. Take your time with each sentence. Let the footage breathe.
